Chick-Fil-A Spicy Chicken Sandwich

Chick-Fil-A Spicy Deluxe Chicken Sandwich Meal

Chick-Fil-A is known for their premium menu of chicken based products, and delicious sides. However, one menu item stuck out to me the most. That item is... the Spicy Deluxe Chicken Sandwich. The Spicy Deluxe Chicken Sandwich is an upgrade to the base version of the Spicy Chicken Sandwich, for it adds pepperjack cheese, lettuce, pickles, and tomatoes to the sandwich, along side the chicken filet. And to top things off, bundled as a meal, I can get a choice of medium or large Drink and side of their signature waffle fries, mac & cheese, etc.

Price: $12.75 USD *Bundled as a Large Meal*
