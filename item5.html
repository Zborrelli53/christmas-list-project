Nintendo™ Wii

The Nintendo™ Wii was a console released in 2006 and was the first console to introduce motion tracking in video games. The Nintendo Wii was packaged with a sensor bar that is plugged into the console itself, and a Wiimote™ controller that has built in sensors that connect to the sensor bar! The console also released with some of the most well known video games including: Wii Sports, Super Mario Galaxy, Mario Kart Wii, and Super Smash Bros. Brawl!

Price: $149.75 USD
